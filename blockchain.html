  2. Blockchain

Blockchain is the next frontier in healthcare that will help solve some of the industry's interoperability challenges. Through our investments and partnerships across a variety of technology enablers, we offer cutting edge solutions.

Its applications in the healthcare industry continue to grow and will disrupt the sector for the betterment of all stakeholders. These include:

  • Improvements to accessing, managing and keeping electronic medical records;
  • Cutting costs and financial process optimization;
  • Enhancing supply chain and authenticating sources, thereby also reducing counterfeit medical products and drugs;
  • Tracking clinical trials and pharmaceuticals;
  • Medical credential tracking and verification;
  • Enhancements to the security and controls of healthcare transactions, for insurance companies and operators;
  • Improvements to patient-doctor interactions;
  • Bolster data security and privacy;
  • Establishment of proper chain of custody in product logistics
  • Chronic disease management and prevention
